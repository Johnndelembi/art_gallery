Hey, I'm Sarafina Sway

I am an artist specialized in painting styles and subjects especially landscape paintings. I have a very strong passion with art. In only year one of experience i have developed a unique artisitic styles that aim to capture the beauty, emotions and essence of the world around me. Most of my paints explore themes of natural environment by using acrylic colors

Each work i create is an invitation for the viewer to engage with the narrative and interpretation i have depicted into the piece.

I am honored to share my artistic vision and excited to connect with those who appreciate the power of visual storytelling through the medium of paint.

Thank you.
